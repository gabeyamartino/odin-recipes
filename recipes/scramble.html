Egg Skillet Scramble

Description

This recipe is both simple and delicious. It only requires a handful of ingredients and can be scaled feed one person, two people, or an entire family! It is a recipe that will not fail. I mean, who doesn't like scrambled eggs?

Before we get started, you'll want to grab a non-stick skillet (I prefer non-stick for easy cleanup) and gather all of your ingredients. This recipe will yield two servings, but as I mentioned earlier, can be scaled to feed as many people as you wish! So, without further ado, let's begin!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1 sausage link (I typically use Aidell's pre-cooked chicken sausage)
