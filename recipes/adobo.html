Chicken Adobo

Descripton

This is a super easy dish that only requires a few ingredients and comes out super tasty! You will essentially just add all of the ingredients into a slow cooker, set it for 2 hours on high (or 4 hours on low) and enjoy when it is done!

Ingredients

  • 3lbs chicken (Thighs work best for this one)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1/2 cup white vinegar
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • Bay leaves
  • Garlic (as much as you'd like)
  • Crushed red pepper

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in your crockpot or slow cooker
  2. Set your crockpot to the low setting
  3. Cover and cook for 4 hours, stirring occasionally (Note, you can also cook for 2.5 on high)
  4. Serve with your favorite side dishes (I usually have it with white rice and steamed brocolli)
