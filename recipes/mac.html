Mac n Cheese

Description

This is just a downright classic. Perfect for a quick lunch, and can also work as a dinner side. The kids will love it, and so will you!

Ingredients

  • 6 cups water
  • 1 package KRAFT Macaroni & Cheese Dinner
  • 1/4 cup margirine
  • 1/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Boil water in a medium sized saucepan
  2. Stir in macaroni; cook 7 to 8 minutes or until tender, stirring occasionally
  3. Drain the water in a collander and return noodles to the saucepan
  4. Add margarine or butter, milk and Cheese Sauce Mix and stir until mixed well
